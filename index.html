Burma 47

Launched within the Chinese 47 compound in 2020, Burma 47 enriches Yangon's breakfast scene by adding authentic Shan and traditional Burmese tea house selections. This outlet quickly became a go-to spot for morning gatherings, offering everything from baby Ei kyar kway to Burmese mohinga, and Shan tofu tote, savored by an increasing number of patrons. In recognition of its outstanding quality, Burma 47 received the Certificate of Excellence Award from foodpanda in 2024.

Pinlon House

Pinlon House, opened in 2020 in the burgeoning North Dagon township, offers a fusion of Thai and Asian cuisines, masterfully crafted by our well-experienced chefs whose passion for authentic flavors is evident in every dish. Designed with families in mind, this venue not only caters to culinary treats but also features live football screenings and an unplugged music setting, making it a perfect destination for casual dining and entertainment.

Chinese 47

Chinese 47 was born out of a passion for innovation in 2018 and is located in the heart of Yangon. As the biggest, busiest, and most spacious among the 47 Group's outlets, it offers classic Chinese dishes with a modern twist from across China. Famous for its Roasted Peking Duck and premium Dim Sum, Chinese 47 is also a top-rated venue for breakfast, customer engagement parties, company event dinners, and birthday parties. Renowned for its exceptional customer service, the restaurant features large private rooms, ensuring comfort and privacy for guests seeking an exclusive dining experience.

Chapter 8

Located in Terminal M Shopping Mall, Mingaladon Township, Chapter 8 opened in 2022 as the ultimate casual dining and nightlife destination. Named for being the eighth restaurant in our group, Chapter 8 features modern decoration and has quickly become a popular beer hangout spot. It offers a range of Thai dishes and bar snacks complemented by an extensive beer and cocktail menu, with both indoor and outdoor seating available. Whether you’re looking to enjoy a lively evening out or a casual meal in a stylish setting, Chapter 8 caters to all.

TORA Modern Izakaya

TORA Modern Izakaya introduces a slice of Japan to Myanmar. Opened in 2023 onInyamyaing Road, it’s become a popular hangout for both locals and expatriates,particularly the Japanese community. This flagship venue of the 47 Group showcases acommitment to authenticity, quality ingredients, and a vibrant izakaya atmosphere.Warayaki (straw-grilled) dishes, particularly aromatic and accompanied by Yakitori, areour signature offerings and have become extremely popular. The venue also features aprivate room with traditional Zashiki seating. Guests can choose from over 60 varietiesof Sake, Shochu, and Japanese Whiskey, making it a premier destination for Japanesecuisine and culture enthusiasts.
